Add Block Rule

Plain domains block all subdomains. Add a path to block just a section (e.g. reddit.com/r/funny, youtube.com/@chan). Use * for wildcards (e.g. *.facebook.com/*). Prefix with kw: to block by page title/body keyword (e.g. kw:gambling — at least 3 chars). For full regex power, prefix with r/ or wrap in /…/ (e.g. r/(facebook|instagram)\.com, /news.*\.html$/). Up to 10 regex rules are active at once.
